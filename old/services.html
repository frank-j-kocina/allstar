Our Products and Services

We offer a wide selection of products for installation, including:
  • Bath Enclosures
  • Mirrors
  • Custom Mirror Frames
  • Glass Products
  • Ventilated Shelving
  • Motorized Screens

Bath Enclosures
Are you ready to update your bathroom? Then let us help. We provide installation of Alumax bath enclosures. They come in a variety of finishes and styles to meet any of your needs.

To view all of the quality products offered by Alumax, Click here.

Mirrors
Whether you are looking for a small beveled mirror or a full wall of mirrors, no job is too big or too small for us! Mirror installation is our specialty. We hand-cut and edge each of our standard order mirrors right on site. If you are looking for something with a beveled edge or a different shape, we can take care of you too! Any mirror that is not a standard order, we can special order it for you. In addition, we coat all of our mirrors with a special edging spray that will prevent peeling and tarnishing in the future.

Custom Mirror Frames
Do you want to transform your bathroom? Do it easily and inexpensively with our custom mirror frames. These frames can be custom cut to fit any size mirror. You'll be sure to find one that suites your tastes, with over 60 different colors and styles to choose from.

Glass Products

Installing glass products is our specialty. We can do just about anything, including:

  • Windows
  • Cabinet Glass
  • Glass Shelving
  • Glass Tabletops
  • Glass Etching

Ventilated Shelving
Are you looking to create some extra storage space? Then let us help you design a custom, Schulte Shelving system that will allow you to maximize your space. We carry Schulte's Lifetime Ventilation Shelving, which is durable, wire shelving, coated in beautiful white epoxy to prevent chipping, fading, and sticking over its lifetime.

Motorized Screens
Want to create some additional living space in your home? Cut down on heating/cooling costs? Or enjoy the Minnesota summer without those pesky mosquitoes? If you answered yes to any of these, then Skeet'r Beat'r is for you! The Skeet'r Beat'r is a motorized, retractable screen that can be mounted on garage doors, entries, porches, and lanais. It is the ultimate screen that will give you privacy, control sun exposure, and keep out insects.
